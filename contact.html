Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your MBTI compatibility analyses?

Our compatibility analyses are based on established MBTI research and personality psychology principles. However, they are for educational and entertainment purposes only and should not be the sole basis for relationship decisions. Every individual is unique, and successful relationships depend on many factors beyond personality type.

Do you offer professional relationship counseling?

No, we do not provide professional counseling or therapy services. MBTILove.xyz is an educational platform focused on personality compatibility insights. For professional relationship advice, we recommend consulting licensed therapists or counselors.

Can I suggest new features or content?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new features, content ideas, or improvements to our existing services. Please use the contact form above with "Feedback" as the subject, and we'll consider your suggestions for future updates.

How do you protect my personal information?

We take privacy seriously and follow strict data protection practices. Please review our detailed Privacy Policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Are you affiliated with the official MBTI organization?

No, MBTILove.xyz is an independent website that provides educational content based on MBTI principles. We are not affiliated with The Myers-Briggs Company or CPP, Inc. For official MBTI assessments, please visit their authorized websites.

Do you offer content in other languages?

Yes! We currently support English, Japanese, and Korean. You can switch languages using the language selector in the top navigation. We're working on expanding to additional languages based on user demand.
